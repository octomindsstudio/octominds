Teppantora isn't just a restaurant; it's a bridge between the serene culinary mastery of Japan and the vibrant dietary landscape of Dhaka. The challenge was unique: how to maintain the absolute authenticity of Japanese Teppanyaki and Sushi while ensuring a 100% Halal-compliant experience.

The digital presence had to reflect this duality — minimal yet bold, traditional yet modern.

---

### The Vision

Step away from the city's noise and immerse yourself in the serenity of Japan. Our Goal was to create a digital gateway that feels as immersive as the restaurant's interior in Uttara.

### Technical Mastery

Building a high-performance restaurant site required a focus on visual weight and load times. With professional food photography being the core driver, we implemented an aggressive image optimization strategy alongside smooth motion triggers.

### Design Language

The visual brand uses a "Dark Studio" palette — charcoal backgrounds that allow the vibrant colors of the sushi and grilled meats to pop. We used a "Signature Red" accent to represent the passion of the chefs and the heat of the Teppan.

<Tabs items={["Typography", "Color Palette", "Motion"]}>
  <Tab value="Typography">
    We used clean, high-contrast sans-serif fonts to ensure readability. The
    headers are bold and geometric, while the body text maintains a light, airy
    feel in line with Japanese minimalism.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Color Palette">
    - **Foundation**: Dark Charcoal (#121212) for a premium, evening-dining
    atmosphere. - **Accent**: Imperial Red (#E63946) for actions and key
    branding elements. - **Surface**: Off-White for high-readability menu
    sections.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Motion">
    Subtle parallax effects on the hero section and staggered reveals for menu
    items create a sense of refined flow as the user explores the offerings.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
