# Precision in Motion.

</Reveal>

AROM (Aroma Fashion Ltd) is more than a manufacturer; it’s a strategic gateway to quality for the global market. Established in 2011 and partnered with Bridge Force Inc. Japan, AROM represents the absolute pinnacle of garment production.

The challenge was to move away from the "industrial catalog" aesthetic and create a cinematic, data-driven narrative that mirrors their commitment to quality and transparency.

---

<Reveal delay={0.2}>

### A Visual Symphony of Production

</Reveal>

We escaped the static nature of manufacturing sites by introducing a liquid, eco-modern aesthetic. Using an Obsidian and Emerald palette, we translated AROM’s ecological commitment into a digital aura that feels both robust and sustainable.

### The "How We Work" Timeline

</Reveal>

We broke down the complexity of high-precision manufacturing into an interactive vertical timeline. From raw fiber selection to the final Japanese-market-ready garment, the journey is presented with clarity and cinematic flow.

<Callout type="note" title="Sustainable Governance">
  As a 100% export-oriented unit, AROM adheres to the strictest environmental
  and social standards, including Sedex and BSCI certifications, all of which
  are documented and visualized within the platform.
</Callout>

### The Visual Language

The design system uses high-contrast typography and glassmorphism elements to create a sense of depth and modernity.

<Tabs items={["Ecosystem", "Navigation", "Optimization"]}>
  <Tab value="Ecosystem">
    We used a "Forest Emerald" foundation to align with their sustainability
    goals, accented by high-visibility safety orange for key metrics.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Navigation">
    A floating "translucent bubble" navigation system keeps the focus on the
    content while providing an app-like, futuristic interaction model.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Optimization">
    Using Framer Motion, every section reveals with a deliberate, high-precision
    stagger that mimics the rhythm of a modern garment facility.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
